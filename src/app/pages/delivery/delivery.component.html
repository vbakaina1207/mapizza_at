Delivery and payment

MA Pizza has its own courier service that delivers pizza around Vienna. You can place an order on the website or call our call center at +43 (097) 18 306 18. Please check in which delivery zone your address is, as this information depends on how quickly our courier can deliver your order. To receive free delivery from MA Pizza, you need to place an order for a minimum amount of € 50.

Green zone
  • The average delivery time is < 29 min
  • Minimum order amount 40 €
Yellow zone
  • The average delivery time is < 59 min
  • Minimum order amount 50 €

Payment

Currently, MA Pizza offers 3 payment methods: online through the LiqPay service, in cash when receiving the order, by card through the terminal when receiving the order. Please notify our manager in advance which payment method  it will be convenient for you if the payment is made directly upon receiving the delivery.

Cash

Pay by cash or card upon receipt by the courier

Courier with terminal

Pay online through the LiqPay service or its analogues

Online payment

Pay online through the LiqPay service or its analogues
